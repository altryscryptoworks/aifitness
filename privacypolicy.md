# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-01-17

AI Fitness ("we," "us," or "our") provides the AI Fitness mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use the App.

If you do not agree with this policy, do not use the App.

## Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly and information generated by your use of the App:

- Account information: Google account ID token, email address, and display name when you sign in.
- Profile and interview data: goals, experience level, schedule preferences, equipment, limitations, and other workout preferences you enter.
- Workout activity: plans generated, sessions completed, sets/reps/weights/RPE, notes, and adherence data.
- Device and usage data: basic app diagnostics (errors, performance, and usage events).

## How We Use Information

We use your information to:

- Create and update personalized workout plans.
- Adapt plans based on your progress and logged workouts.
- Maintain and secure your account.
- Improve app performance, reliability, and user experience.

## How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We only share information:

- With service providers that help us operate the App (hosting, analytics, AI services).
- When required by law or to protect our rights and users.
- With your consent.

## AI Services

We use AI services to generate and update workout plans. The data you provide (goals, preferences, and workout logs) may be sent to AI service providers to produce your plan. We do not include your passwords in AI requests.

## Data Retention

We keep your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the App. You can delete workout plans and logs from within the App, and you can request full deletion at any time.

## Your Choices

You can:

- Update your profile and interview answers in the App.
- Delete your workout plans and logs in the App.
- Request data export or deletion by contacting us (see Contact).

## Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.
